Make sure that you have purchased Trezor One or Trezor Model T and you have a PC as these are the primary needs when you think to store crypto offline. Now, if you have a Trezor device then you can start setting up your Trezor Wallet account with the help of the next sections of this post.

HOW TO INSTALL TREZOR SUITE ON YOUR PC?

Users who are looking to start setting up a new Trezor account need to follow the Trezor Suite installation path. Now, approach the steps that are given below to install Trezor Suite on your device:

  1. Open a browser and then visit https://trezor.io/ page
  2. Now, you need to choose the ‘Suite’ option from the top-right corner
  3. Get to the ‘Get the Desktop app’ option and click on it
  4. Don’t forget to choose the OS version of your device
  5. Now, wait for a while and then click on the downloaded file
  6. Finally, Trezor Suite will be installed on your Windows or Mac

A quick guide to creating a Trezor account

Trezor allows you to set up your online account as well by keeping users’ identities anonymous. This account helps you to add cryptocurrencies to your account. Now, follow these steps to create a new Trezor Wallet account:

  1. On your laptop, launch the Trezor Suite app
  2. Connect the Trezor device to the laptop
  3. Reach to the ‘Create a new wallet’ option and click on it
  4. After that, you need to go to the next page and choose the password for your wallet
  5. Now, click ‘Next’ to visit the next page and reveal the recovery phrase details
  6. Once you find the recovery phrases, click the ‘Next’ button
  7. Now, you are asked to verify the seed phrase on this page
  8. Type the required sequence of phrases and then click the ‘Finish’ button

Method to login to Trezor account with password

Password can help you to access your Trezor account in case you are locked out of your wallet. The steps that will let you access Trezor Wallet with a password are listed below:

  1. Launch Trezor Suite on your Windows or Mac
  2. Now, you will be prompted to enter the password
  3. Type the Trezor account password details correctly
  4. After typing the password, click the ‘Unlock’ button to access the wallet

How to restore my Trezor Wallet on a new device?

In case you have lost the device on which you were accessed Trezor Wallet, you need to follow the wallet restoration process that is given below:

  1. On your device, launch the Trezor Suite app
  2. Now, connect your Trezor and click the ‘I have secret recovery phrase’ button
  3. After that, type the recovery phrase and then enter the password details
  4. Now, you need to recheck the password and then click the ‘Restore’ button
